Top 3 Facts about NZ

1. Unique Wildlife

New Zealand is home to many unique species found nowhere else on Earth, such as the kiwi bird, kakapo parrot, and tuatara lizard.

2. Geothermal Activity

The country has abundant geothermal activity, with hot springs, geysers, and mud pools, particularly in the North Island.

3. Stunning Landscapes

New Zealand boasts diverse and breathtaking landscapes, from fjords and mountains to beaches and forests.
