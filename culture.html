Top 3 Things about the Culture of NZ

1. Maori Culture

The indigenous Maori culture is a significant part of New Zealand's identity, with its own language, traditions, and art forms.

They have been through lots of changes over the years, but they have managed to preserve their cultural heritage.

2. Outdoor Lifestyle

New Zealanders, also known as Kiwis, have a strong connection with the wild life and the outdoors.
They enjoy activities such as hiking, camping, and water sports, which are an integral part of their lifestyle.

3. Community Spirit

New Zealanders are known for their friendly and welcoming nature, with a strong sense of community and social cohesion.

They value inclusivity and diversity, making it a great place to live and visit.
